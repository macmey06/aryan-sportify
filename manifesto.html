What I stand for

THE MANIFESTO

7 honest commitments. No empty promises.

01
🏆
SPORTIFY House Cup

A year-long running trophy — points from every monthly inter-house event accumulate, one house crowned champion at the end of the year.

"One year. Every event. One champion house."
02
💜
SPORTIFY Her

One reserved spot for a girl in every team, in every event — because inclusion should be built into every game, not an afterthought.

"Every team, every event — a seat always reserved for SPORTIFY Her."
03
🏠
Monthly Inter-House Events

Every month, a new online competition between houses. Keeps the community engaged and competitive throughout the term.

"Every month, a new game. Every house, a new chance."
04
📰
Newsletter

Monthly newsletter covering event recaps, upcoming calendar, student sports spotlight and winners. Released consistently every month.

"Your monthly dose of everything SPORTIFY."
05
🤝
Mixed Level Teams

Every event team will have at least one Foundation or Diploma student — creating natural senior-junior bonds through sport.

"Every level, every team, every event."
06
📺
Watch Parties

Organised watch parties on G Meet for big matches — IPL, World Cup. Casual, fun, just students enjoying sport together.

"Big matches, better together."
07
🌍
City Sports Meetups

Regular sports meetups happening across cities — different sport every time, just show up and play.

"Same city, same turf, same love for sport."
